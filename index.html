Aditya Arora

Research Engineer · IIAI, Abu Dhabi, UAE · aditya.arora@inceptioniai.org

I am a Research Engineer at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence since 2018. Each day, I tend to find solutions to problems at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Before my current position, I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science Engineering from DIT University. I have also been a Research Intern at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee for 6 months under Dr. Balasubramanian Raman. My research interests include low-level vision tasks such as Image Denoising, Image Enhancement and Super Resolution.
